BMW Legacy & History

Over a century of automotive innovation

1916

The Beginning

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke) founded in Munich, Germany. Originally an aircraft engine manufacturer, establishing engineering excellence from the start.

1928

First Automobile

BMW acquires Fahrzeugfabrik Eisenach and begins automobile production with the Dixi 3/15, based on the Austin 7.

1972

BMW M Division

BMW Motorsport GmbH established, creating the legendary M Series - the ultimate driving machines combining performance and luxury.

2007

Project i Begins

BMW launches Project i, a think tank dedicated to developing sustainable mobility solutions, leading to the revolutionary i-Series electric vehicles.

2023

Neue Klasse

Announcement of the "Neue Klasse" - BMW's vision for the future of electric mobility with next-generation technology, sustainability, and digital experiences.

BMW Today

Today, BMW stands at the forefront of automotive innovation, blending over 100 years of engineering excellence with cutting-edge technology. From the iconic 3 Series to the revolutionary i-Series electric vehicles, BMW continues to define the ultimate driving experience while pioneering sustainable mobility for future generations.
